AI Agents Powering SecureID

Every SecureID product has dedicated Agentic AI agents that observe, plan, act, and learn — continuously.

NFC Sync Agent

Goal: Maintain card-cloud consistency on every tap

Ensures data on the physical NFC card matches the cloud record after every interaction. Handles conflict resolution, offline queuing, and delta sync. Achieves 99.5% consistency rate across all deployed cards.

LeadIntel Agent

Goal: Score, nurture, and convert leads from Tap-to-Share

Analyzes tap interactions and microsite visits to score lead quality. Triggers automated WhatsApp follow-ups, books meetings, and syncs with CRM. Delivers 3x improvement in lead conversion rates.

Compliance Agent

Goal: Continuous regulatory monitoring across all card services

Monitors key management processes against GlobalPlatform standards. Validates cryptographic operations, audits access logs, generates compliance reports. Flags deviations before they become violations.

Identity Agent

Goal: Detect fraud and streamline eKYC verification

AI-powered identity verification using biometrics, document analysis, and behavioral patterns. Detects fraudulent card usage in real-time. Manages eKYC workflows and maintains audit trails for regulatory compliance.
