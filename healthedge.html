AI Agents Powering HealthEdge

Every HealthEdge product has dedicated Agentic AI agents that observe, plan, act, and learn — continuously.

HealthScreen AI

Goal: Detect cancer indicators in medical images

Runs CNN-based analysis on mammograms, X-rays, and oral images. Assigns risk scores. Prioritizes high-risk cases. Tracks false positive/negative rates and self-improves.

VitaGuard Agent

Goal: Detect early warning signs before critical events

Monitors daily vitals via connected devices. Builds personal baselines. Detects anomalies using time-series analysis. Escalates through patient → guardian → emergency chain.

Deployment Agent

Goal: Optimize mobile screening unit routes

Analyzes screening history, incidence heatmaps, population density, and seasonal patterns. Recommends weekly deployment schedules. 40% improvement in detection rates.

Triage AI Agent

Goal: Prioritize patients and route to correct care

AI-powered chatbot performs initial triage based on symptoms. Routes to appropriate specialist. Provides clinical decision support to doctors in real-time.

Follow-up Agent

Goal: Ensure no patient falls through the cracks

Tracks referrals, pending lab results, medication adherence. Sends WhatsApp reminders. Flags patients who miss appointments. Manages continuum of care calendar.

Compliance Agent

Goal: Monitor regulatory adherence across platforms

Scans workflows against HL7/FHIR standards. Validates data privacy. Generates audit reports. Flags deviations before they become violations.
