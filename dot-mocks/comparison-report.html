⭐ Our Unique Advantages (Not in Competitors)

🎤 Voice Commands

8 hands-free spoken commands for recording, snapshot, source switching and navigation. Neither Olympus nor Proximie offer this. Critical for sterile environments where touch is restricted.

📄 PDF Surgery Report

One-click PDF generation with procedure details, captured clips, surgeon notes and hospital branding. Unique feature — no competitor offers this out of the box.

📱 Mobile-First Responsive Design

Full functionality on smartphones and tablets. Olympus ENDOALPHA is hardware-locked; Proximie has limited mobile support. Enables ward-round review and on-call access.

⌨ Keyboard Shortcuts (7 keys)

Space, M, S, R, 1/2/3, V, ? — complete keyboard control for power users. Not available in either competitor platform.

🧠 AI Surgery Copilot Panel

Step-by-step surgical phase tracking with confidence scores and risk indicators. Olympus has no AI layer; Proximie's AI is post-operative analytics only.

📺 All-Monitors Grid View

Simultaneous view of all 3 camera sources in one screen. Proximie lacks this; Olympus has it only via dedicated hardware displays, not in the software UI.

✎ Live Annotation on Feed (v1.1)

Pen, arrow, and circle drawing tools directly on the live video. Proximie has annotation but requires their cloud platform. Ours works locally with no external dependency — and Olympus has nothing equivalent.

🔒 In-App Security Bar (v1.1)

Persistent AES-256 + HIPAA compliance indicators visible on every screen. No competitor surfaces security status inline — this is a strong trust signal during hospital procurement demos.
