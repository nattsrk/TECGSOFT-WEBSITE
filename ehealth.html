AI-Enhanced Clinical Features

Key Features

Everything a primary health facility needs to go fully paperless — from patient registration to pharmacy dispensing.

Unique Health ID

Tags all health records across facilities. One patient, one identity — searchable across every clinic, van, and screening unit in the network.

Electronic Medical Records

Primary and secondary symptom capture with structured data entry. Complete patient history, diagnosis, treatment plans, and clinical notes in one place.

Virtual Consultation

Multi-channel, low-bandwidth video and audio consultation. Connects rural health posts with specialist doctors in urban centers — works even on 2G.

Connected Devices

BP, SpO2, Pulse, Temperature, and Glucometer readings captured automatically via Bluetooth. Zero manual entry, zero transcription errors.

Digital Lab Management

Supply chain tracking for consumables and medicines. Lab sample collection, result entry, and report generation — all digitized end to end.

Pharmacy Management

Prescription fulfillment and tracking. Stock management, dispensing logs, expiry alerts, and patient medication history in a unified workflow.

Special Programs

Smart Appointment Scheduling

End-to-End Clinical Journey

How It Works

A complete paperless workflow from the moment a patient walks in to post-visit AI-triggered follow-ups.

1

Patient Walk-in

Registration and Unique Health ID assignment. Existing patients identified instantly via biometric or ID lookup.

2

Clinic Doctor Consultation

Vitals captured via Bluetooth-connected devices. Symptoms recorded. AI-assisted clinical decision support in real-time.

3

Specialty Consult / Lab Referral / Medicine

Doctor routes patient to specialist consultation, lab tests, or direct medication based on diagnosis.

4

Virtual Consultation over Video

Low-bandwidth video/audio call with remote specialist. Works on 2G networks with adaptive quality streaming.

5

Lab Sample → Results → Doctor Follow-up

Sample collected and tracked digitally. Results auto-routed to prescribing doctor. Abnormal values flagged by AI.

6

Prescription → Pharmacy → Patient

Digital prescription sent to on-site pharmacy. Medicines dispensed and logged. Patient receives medication with instructions.

7

Case Sheet Updated → AI Follow-up Triggered

Complete case sheet saved. AI Follow-up Agent schedules reminders, tracks medication adherence, and ensures continuum of care.

Autonomous Clinical Intelligence

Agentic AI Inside eHealth+

Three specialized AI agents that observe, reason, act, and learn — continuously improving clinical outcomes.

Triage AI Agent

Goal: Analyze symptoms, route patients, support clinical decisions

Performs AI-powered initial triage based on reported symptoms and vitals. Routes patients to the correct specialist. Provides real-time Clinical Decision Support System (CDSS) recommendations to doctors during consultation.

Symptom Analysis Patient Routing CDSS

Appointment Agent

Goal: Optimize scheduling, minimize wait times, handle cancellations

AI-optimized appointment scheduling that balances doctor availability, patient urgency, and facility capacity. Handles cancellations and rebooking automatically. Learns from historical patterns to predict demand.

Smart Scheduling Auto-Rebooking Demand Prediction

Follow-up Agent

Goal: Ensure no patient falls through the cracks

Tracks missed appointments, pending lab results, and medication adherence. Sends WhatsApp reminders to patients. Flags overdue follow-ups for doctors. Manages the entire continuum of care calendar automatically.

Missed Appointment Tracking WhatsApp Reminders Care Continuum
