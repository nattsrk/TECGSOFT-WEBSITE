Autonomous Agentic AI — Always On

Detect. Alert. Save Lives.

Goal

Detect early warning signs of cardiac events, strokes, and diabetic crises — 24 to 48 hours before they become critical.

How It Works

Monitors daily vitals from connected devices. Builds a personal baseline for each patient over time. Detects deviations using time-series anomaly detection algorithms. The longer it monitors, the smarter it gets.

Impact

24-48 hours early warning before critical events

2 Patient App → In-app alert
