Our Story

We started by building secure smart card platforms for India's national health insurance programs — RSBY, TNHIS/CMCHIS, and Bhamashah. SCOSTA-compliant cards with in-house key management systems. We wrote the applets, designed the key ceremonies, and issued cards to millions of citizens across the country. Security wasn't a feature — it was the foundation.

Then we took that expertise to Africa. We built eHealth platforms, deployed mobile clinics, and launched cancer screening programs across Senegal and Rwanda. Over 2,000 health posts digitized. More than 10 million video consultations delivered. From rural villages with no internet to national-scale hospital networks — we built systems that worked everywhere.

Today, every platform we build runs on Agentic AI. Autonomous agents that process insurance claims, screen medical images, score sales leads, sync NFC cards, and optimize operations — without waiting for instructions. We didn't bolt AI onto legacy systems. We re-engineered everything from the ground up, with agents at the core.
