Camp Workflow

End-to-end screening camp process — from pre-registration to government health repository upload.

1

WhatsApp Pre-registration + Walk-in

Patients register via WhatsApp bot or walk in directly at the camp. Pre-registered patients get priority queue.

2

Registration / Revisit with Health ID

Unique Health ID created or existing ID linked. Demographics, consent, and medical history captured digitally.

3

Basic Vitals Capture

BP, SpO2, Pulse, Temperature, Height & Weight — all captured via Bluetooth-connected devices and auto-synced to the patient record.

4

Doctor Checks → Advises Screening

On-site doctor reviews vitals, medical history, and risk factors. Recommends appropriate screening tests based on age, gender, and history.

5

Age/Gender Screening Workflow

Intelligent routing based on patient profile — Child path: growth metrics, basic checks. Female path: mammogram, Pap smear, oral screening. Male path: chest X-ray, oral screening.

6

AI Analyzes Images → Risk Scoring

HealthScreen AI runs CNN-based analysis on all captured images in real-time. Assigns risk scores. High-risk cases flagged for immediate attention.

HealthScreen AI Active
7

Backend Team Verifies → Report to Patient Mobile

Specialist team reviews AI findings, confirms diagnosis, generates final report. Report delivered to patient via SMS/WhatsApp with next steps.

8

Continuum of Care Calendar Triggered

Automated follow-up calendar activated. WhatsApp reminders for re-screening, referral appointments, and treatment tracking. No patient lost to follow-up.

9

Records Uploaded to Government Health Repository

All screening data, reports, and outcomes uploaded to national health repositories (NHA, ABDM). Contributes to population-level cancer surveillance data.

ABDM / Ayushman Bharat Compliant

AI Agents Powering WoW Screening

Two autonomous AI agents work together — one analyzes medical images, the other optimizes where and when to deploy screening units.

Agent 1 of 2

HealthScreen AI Agent

Goal: Detect cancer indicators in medical images with high accuracy

Capabilities

• CNN-based image analysis on mammograms, X-rays, oral images
• Automated risk scoring — Low / Medium / High
• Prioritizes high-risk cases for immediate review
• Self-improving — learns from confirmed diagnoses
• Tracks false positive/negative rates continuously

Impact

Reduces missed diagnoses significantly. Reporting turnaround reduced from days to hours. Enables real-time screening at the point of care.
